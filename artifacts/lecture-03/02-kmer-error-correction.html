k-mer Error Correction

Every read brings some noise. Break reads into k-mers and the frequency spectrum splits into two populations: a tall peak around the mean coverage (true k-mers) and a spike at frequency 1–2 (errors). A threshold between them lets you flag likely errors. Drop coverage too low and the populations merge — no threshold can separate them.
