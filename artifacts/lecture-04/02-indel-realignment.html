INDEL Realignment Demo

A true 2-bp deletion, viewed through a naive aligner that cannot open gaps, looks like a cluster of five false SNVs right next to one another. Local realignment reopens the window, lets the gap shift where it belongs, and the mismatch cluster collapses into a single clean DEL call. Toggle the alignment mode to watch the same read data produce completely different variant calls.

The reads are simulated from a donor that carries a known INDEL; the reference is the unchanged template. The window slider controls how much flanking context the visualisation shows around the variant site.
