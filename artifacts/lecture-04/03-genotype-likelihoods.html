Genotype Likelihood Calculator

Given a pileup of read bases and their Phred qualities, compute P(D|G) for each of the three diploid genotypes, multiply by a prior P(G), renormalise, and read off the MAP call. Swap the prior from diploid-human to somatic-low-frequency on the 15% VAF preset — the MAP flips from 0/0 to 0/1, because the prior decides what counts as evidence.

Computation

1. Likelihood P(D|G)

2. Prior P(G)

3. Posterior P(G|D)

Per-base likelihood: for a homozygous genotype with allele a, P(b|G) = (1−ε) if b=a else ε/3 with ε=10−q/10. For a heterozygote with alleles (a1,a2), P(b|G) = ½·P(b|hom a1) + ½·P(b|hom a2). Read likelihoods sum in log-space. Posterior ∝ likelihood × prior, then renormalise. GQ = −10·log10(Prunner-up/PMAP), capped at 99.
