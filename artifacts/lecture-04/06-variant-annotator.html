Variant Annotator

A VCF row is just a coordinate and two letters. Annotation turns it into a verdict. Step a variant through the four layers — gene context (VEP/snpEff), population frequency (gnomAD), clinical evidence (ClinVar), and functional prediction (REVEL/CADD/SpliceAI) — and watch the same pipeline produce radically different verdicts. Compare the intergenic common SNP preset with TP53 R175H to see layered evidence in action.
