At strength 0 the cloud is the raw MLE — high-scatter, many genes with implausibly tiny or huge dispersion. At strength 1 everything collapses onto the DESeq2-style trend α(μ) = α₀ + α₁/μ. The interesting region is in between: the shrunk cloud hugs the trend without erasing the genes that genuinely disagree with it. Those are the outlier dots that refuse to slide down their arrow — they carry biology worth keeping.
