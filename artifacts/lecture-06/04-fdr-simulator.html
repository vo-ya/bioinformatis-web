FDR Simulator — Bonferroni vs BH

Simulate a genome-scale experiment with a controlled fraction of true positives, then compare what Bonferroni and Benjamini–Hochberg call significant at the same threshold. The p-value histogram shows the null uniform floor plus the spike of real signal near zero. Watch empirical FDR track the declared rate for BH while Bonferroni spends most of its power keeping FDR near zero.
