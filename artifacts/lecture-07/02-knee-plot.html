Every droplet gets a barcode. Most barcodes are ambient — empty droplets that caught a whiff of background mRNA. Sort all barcodes by UMI count and you see a cliff: real cells plateau high, ambient plateaus low, with an unstable knee in between. Move the threshold to decide what counts as a cell. Stretch the ambient population and notice the knee stays put — it is about UMI separability, not absolute droplet counts.
