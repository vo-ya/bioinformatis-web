Normalisation Comparison

One gene, three lenses. A simulated 500-cell dataset (T cells, B cells, monocytes) is drawn with per-cell sequencing depth varying over an order of magnitude. The same gene is then shown as raw counts, log-normalised counts (log1p(count / total × 1e4)), and Pearson residuals (SCTransform-style, θ = 100). The aha is watching depth-driven bleed collapse from left to right.
