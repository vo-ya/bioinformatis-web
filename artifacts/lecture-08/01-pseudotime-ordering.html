Pseudotime Ordering Demo

Cells sit on a developmental trajectory with a known true time (from simulation). We fit a principal curve through the 2D cloud and read each cell's pseudotime as its arc-length along the curve. Kendall τ scores how well the inferred ordering recovers the true one. The aha: when noise is low and the trajectory is a simple arc the ordering tracks truth almost perfectly; crank noise up or pick an ambiguous shape and the curve starts to confuse cells that live near each other but are far apart in real time.
