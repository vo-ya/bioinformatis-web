RNA Velocity Vector Field

Each cell carries both spliced (mature) and unspliced (nascent) counts for every gene. The steady-state ratio u = gamma·s defines equilibrium; cells above the line are actively inducing, below are repressing. Projecting v = beta·u − gamma·s onto a 2D embedding gives a flow field from immature to mature cells. Turn cell-cycle genes on to see the documented failure mode.
