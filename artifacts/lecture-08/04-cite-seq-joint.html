CITE-seq Joint Embedding

Five true cell types are consistent across modalities, but a sixth group is discrepant — its RNA looks like type A while its proteins look like type B (a stand-in for post-transcriptional regulation or protein degradation). Compare three UMAPs: RNA-only, protein-only, and a WNN joint embedding that fuses the two kNN graphs with per-cell weights.
