Ligand-Receptor Network Browser

Simulated single-cell expression for five immune & stromal cell types is scored against a curated ligand-receptor database. Each directed edge is a cell-type → cell-type communication axis, coloured by ligand family, weighted by the geometric-mean expression score. Move the threshold slider and the network thins; filter by family and only those colours remain. The aha: cell-cell communication graphs are extremely sensitive to the score cutoff you pick.
