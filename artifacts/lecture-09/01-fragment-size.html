Fragment-Size Distribution Explorer

ATAC-seq fragment-size distributions carry a fingerprint of the chromatin they came from. Tn5 hops into open DNA, then hops again — the distance between cuts is either sub-nucleosomal (naked linker), one wrap of a nucleosome, two wraps, three wraps. Plot the distribution on log-x and you see a ladder. Break your protocol and the ladder vanishes. ChIP-seq, which sonicates pre-fragmented chromatin, never had a ladder in the first place.
