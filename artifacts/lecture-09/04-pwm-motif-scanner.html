PWM Motif Scanner

Slide a position weight matrix across a DNA sequence. Each window yields a log-odds score; positions above threshold are called hits. The aha is threshold-sensitivity: a strong PWM on a planted site gives a handful of clean hits, but drop the cutoff and spurious windows everywhere light up. The random-null expectation shows how easily chance alone matches a short motif.
