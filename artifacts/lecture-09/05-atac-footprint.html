ATAC Footprint Analyser

Tn5 cuts open chromatin. Where a transcription factor sits on DNA, it blocks Tn5, leaving a small dip flanked by accessibility peaks — a "footprint". Each motif instance is individually far too noisy to see it. Aggregate over N sites and the dip emerges as 1/√N noise beats a constant signal. Crank N down and watch the footprint dissolve into Poisson noise.
