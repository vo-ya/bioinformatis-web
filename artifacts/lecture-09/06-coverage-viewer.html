ChIP vs ATAC Coverage Viewer

Walk a ~20 kb genomic window and compare signal shape across four assays: CTCF ChIP, H3K4me3, H3K27me3, and ATAC-seq. Switch scenarios to see how the same four tracks rearrange themselves over an active promoter, a silenced domain, a cluster of enhancers, and an active gene body. The aha is that no single track diagnoses regulatory state — the combination does.
