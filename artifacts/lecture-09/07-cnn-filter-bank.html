CNN Filter Bank Visualiser

A toy model of Enformer's first convolutional layer. Eight 8-bp filters are each initialised to resemble a canonical TF motif (TATA, GC-box, AP1, NFkB, CTCF, E-box, SP1, poly-A). Feed in a DNA sequence and see per-filter activation tracks along the sequence. The aha: a CNN filter bank is a PWM scan in parallel — each filter is a matched template, each track is its scan output.
