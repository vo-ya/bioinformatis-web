Feature guide

Diagonal
Adjacent bins contact each other often. Dominates raw counts.
TAD triangles
Square blocks on the diagonal; same-TAD pairs enriched over baseline.
Compartment checkerboard
Off-diagonal plaid of A-A and B-B enrichments; only visible after detrending.
Focal loops
Isolated bright pixels away from the diagonal; chromatin loops.
