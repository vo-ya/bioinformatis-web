SV Resolution · Short vs Long Reads

Plant a structural variant of your choice inside a 100 kb window, then drop Illumina paired-end (150 bp) and PacBio HiFi (~18 kb) reads on it. A read that fully spans the breakpoints resolves the SV directly; a short read that straddles only one breakpoint leaves split/discordant evidence that still works but is indirect. Watch how HiFi saturates recall for mid-size SVs while Illumina collapses once the event outruns its insert size.

Default preset (5 kb deletion, 30×): many HiFi reads blow straight through both breakpoints, while Illumina contributes a handful of split reads and almost no direct spans — the 300–500 bp insert can only bracket, not bridge, a 5 kb event. Push SV size to 25 kb and even HiFi thins out; you would now want ONT ultra-long. Shrink SV size below 300 bp and Illumina catches up via split-read evidence.
