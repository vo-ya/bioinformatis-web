GFA Format Decoder

GFA (Graphical Fragment Assembly) is a tab-separated text serialisation of a sequence graph. Every line is a record — H header, S segment (a node with a DNA sequence), L link (a directed edge joining two segment ends), P path (an ordered walk of oriented segments), W walk (the GFA 1.1 haplotype form). Paste or pick a preset below, and watch the text become a graph. Remove an L line, break the paths. Drop a field from an S line, get a line-numbered error.
