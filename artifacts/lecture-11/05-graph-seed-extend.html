Graph Seed-and-Extend Walker

Align a read to a small DAG reference by placing k-mer seeds on matching nodes, then extending base-by-base along outgoing edges. At every branch the algorithm forks into parallel candidate paths and tracks running scores; the winner solidifies while losers dim. Graph alignment is linear alignment plus branching decisions.
