Coalescent Tree Simulator

Simulate Kingman's standard coalescent for a sample of n lineages drawn from an effective population of size Ne. Waiting times between coalescence events are exponential with rate (k choose 2)/(2Ne), so the last pair takes the longest. Increase n and watch the TMRCA barely budge — doubling Ne doubles it.
