PSMC Demographic Reconstructor

Simulate a single diploid genome under a user-defined demographic history Ne(t), then run a simplified PSMC-style HMM that treats the per-window coalescent time as a hidden state and inverts per-bin rates into Ne(t). The recovered curve tracks ancient bottlenecks well, but loses resolution in the very recent and very ancient past — the signal simply isn't there from one diploid.
