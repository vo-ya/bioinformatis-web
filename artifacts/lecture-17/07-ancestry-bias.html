Educational simulation. The mapping from training-corpus fraction to per-ancestry F1 uses a calibrated power-law curve approximating published disparity magnitudes (e.g. Manolio 2009; Cheng 2023; ClinGen Ancestry WG reports). Real F1 also depends on variant type, allele frequency, and gene-specific factors. Not a benchmark of any specific predictor's actual performance.
