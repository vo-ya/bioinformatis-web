Somatic Variant Caller Simulator

Simulate a tumour-normal pair at user-set tumour purity, sequencing depth, and subclone structure. Call somatic variants with simplified Mutect2-style logic and observe what's detected vs missed vs false-positive. Aha: tumour purity and coverage depth together determine which subclones are detectable; one without the other is not enough.

Educational simulation. The variant generator uses synthetic VAFs derived from simple binomial sampling; the caller applies a minimum-VAF threshold + binomial significance test. Not a faithful Mutect2 reproduction. Sequencer error rate fixed at 0.1%; FFPE and PoN filters not modelled.
