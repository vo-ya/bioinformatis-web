Mutational Signature Decomposer

Build a synthetic 96-bin tumour mutation spectrum as a non-negative mixture of 2–4 COSMIC signatures. Run a simplified NMF decomposition (multiplicative-update rules) and compare recovered exposures vs ground truth. Aha: NMF is mathematically straightforward; the hard part is choosing $K$ — the model-order-selection problem.
