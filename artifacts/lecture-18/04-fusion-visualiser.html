Gene Fusion Visualiser

Pick a canonical cancer gene fusion. The visualiser shows the parent genes, the breakpoint, the fusion transcript, and the fusion-protein domain architecture. Aha: oncogenic fusions are modular — pair an oligomerisation domain (or active promoter) with a kinase domain and you get a constitutively-active driver.

Educational visualisation. Domain boundaries simplified; clinically validated tools (Arriba, STAR-Fusion, FusionCatcher) plus DNA-side SV callers (GRIDSS, Manta) drive real fusion calling.
