AMP/ASCO/CAP Oncology Tier Classifier

Pick a somatic variant + cancer type. The classifier looks up the variant-cancer-drug evidence in a curated mini-database (a slice of OncoKB / CIViC) and returns the AMP/ASCO/CAP tier + recommended action. Aha: oncology tiering is a deterministic lookup keyed by (variant × cancer × drug evidence) — different in shape from ACMG/AMP germline aggregation.
