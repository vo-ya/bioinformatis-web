NJ vs ML Tree Comparator

Pick a dataset; the artifact shows the NJ tree (with bootstrap support) and the ML tree side by side. Aha: NJ is fast and usually correct; ML is more rigorous; both agree on well-resolved branches but can differ at hard branches with low support.
