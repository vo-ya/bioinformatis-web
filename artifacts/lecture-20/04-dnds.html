dN/dS Calculator

Pick a gene preset; the calculator computes ω = dN/dS plus a likelihood-ratio test against the neutral null (ω = 1). Aha: dN/dS quantifies selection — purifying (< 1), neutral (≈ 1), positive (> 1); LRT distinguishes the regimes.
