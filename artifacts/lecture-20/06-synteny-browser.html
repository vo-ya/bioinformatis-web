Educational simulation. Synteny dot plots in production come from MUMmer (DNA), MCScanX (gene-level), or LASTZ (chained alignments). The point distributions here are calibrated against published genome comparisons but simplified; real plots have far more noise and gene-density variation. Not a benchmark.
