Viterbi Stepper

Step through Viterbi cell-by-cell on a 3-state HMM trellis with 8-character input. Each step shows the max-plus computation for one cell. Aha: Viterbi is bottom-up max-plus DP; pointers store the argmax for backtrace.

Educational simulation. The HMM parameters are hand-set for clarity (small state count, peaked emissions). Real Viterbi runs over chromosome-scale tapes with 100s of states (gene finders) or 100,000s of states (profile HMMs). Not a benchmark.
