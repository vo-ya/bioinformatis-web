Spectral Clustering Visualizer

On a 30-node network with 3 ground-truth communities, project onto the 2nd and 3rd smallest eigenvectors of the Laplacian; k-means cluster in eigenspace. Aha: low-frequency eigenvectors separate communities; k-means in eigenspace recovers them.

Educational simulation. The Laplacian eigendecomposition is computed in-browser via simple power iteration on the small (30×30) matrix. Real spectral clustering uses ARPACK / scipy.sparse.linalg for large sparse Laplacians; networkx and scikit-learn provide production implementations. Not a benchmark.
