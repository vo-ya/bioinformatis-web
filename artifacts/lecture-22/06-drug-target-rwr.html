Educational simulation. Drug-target rankings are precomputed via RWR on a hand-built heterogeneous toy network (~50 drugs, ~100 targets, ~30 diseases). Held-out targets are real published drug-target associations excluded from the seed set for the validation demo. Real production uses STITCH, DrugBank, and STRING + RWR or GNN-based methods. Not for clinical use.
