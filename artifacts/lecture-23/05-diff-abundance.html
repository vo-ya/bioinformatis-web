Educational simulation. Hand-crafted 30-sample dataset where 5 of 50 taxa are truly differentially abundant; the rest move "apparently" due to compositional bias. P-values are simulated to reflect typical t-test (over-significant), DESeq2 (slightly better), and ANCOM-BC (correctly few hits) behavior. Real implementations: ANCOM-BC R package, DESeq2 (Love 2014), MaAsLin2 (Mallick 2021). Not a benchmark.
