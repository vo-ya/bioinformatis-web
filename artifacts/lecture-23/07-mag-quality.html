Educational simulation. Generates marker-gene presence patterns matching MIMAG-defined quality categories. Real CheckM uses ~120 lineage-specific markers from Hug 2016 / Parks 2015; CheckM2 uses machine-learning-based estimates. GTDB-Tk taxonomic placement uses average nucleotide identity (ANI) + phylogenetic placement. Not a benchmark.
