Mock Vina Docking Simulator

Generate 9 candidate poses inside a synthetic pocket, score them with a simplified Vina-style energy function (vdW + H-bond + hydrophobic + rotor penalty), and rank. Aha: best Vina pose is usually within ±2 kcal/mol of the true binding mode — but rank #1 vs #3 is essentially noise.

Educational simulator. Replicates the shape of Vina output (clustered poses, ΔG in kcal/mol, RMSD between poses) using a synthetic energy function — does not run an actual Vina computation. Real Vina is open source: github.com/ccsb-scripps/AutoDock-Vina. Not for clinical use.
