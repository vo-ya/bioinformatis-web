ADMET Profile Predictor

Tweak physicochemical properties of a candidate molecule and see how 8 ADMET endpoints (absorption, distribution, metabolism, hERG, AMES, CYP3A4, BBB, hepatotox) shift. Aha: most ADMET endpoints are correlated — fixing one (e.g. lowering logP for solubility) often breaks another (permeability).

Educational predictor. Uses synthetic logistic functions over physicochemical inputs to mimic the direction of effect for each endpoint — actual predictors (ADMETlab, SwissADME, pkCSM) train models on tens of thousands of measurements and produce more accurate but still imperfect predictions. Not a substitute for in vitro / in vivo experiments. Not for clinical use.
