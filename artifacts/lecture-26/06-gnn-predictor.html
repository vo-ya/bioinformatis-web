GNN Property Predictor (toy)

Train a tiny synthetic message-passing model to predict logS (aqueous solubility) on a small dataset, and compare random vs. scaffold splits. Aha: random splits inflate test R² by 5–15 points; scaffold splits give the realistic out-of-domain performance you'll see in production.
