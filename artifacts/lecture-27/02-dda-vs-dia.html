DDA vs DIA Acquisition Simulator

Simulate three replicate LC-MS/MS runs on a 1,000-peptide library; compare DDA top-N selection vs DIA window scanning. Aha: DDA's top-N stochastic selection produces ~70% replicate concordance; DIA's deterministic window scan jumps that to ~85%+.

—
Educational simulator. Models a 1,000-peptide library with log-normal abundances and per-replicate sampling noise. The shape (DDA stochastic, DIA deterministic) and rough concordance numbers (~70% DDA, ~85% DIA) match published comparisons (Bruderer 2017; Demichev 2020). Not a substitute for actual instrument data. Not for clinical use.
