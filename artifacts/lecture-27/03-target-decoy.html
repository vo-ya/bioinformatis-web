Educational simulator. Generates 10,000 target + 10,000 decoy PSM scores from gaussian mixtures with adjustable mean separation; the FDR formula matches the standard target-decoy estimator: FDR = #decoys ≥ t / #targets ≥ t. Real distributions are skewed, often log-normal; modern tools (Percolator, MS2Rescore) use ML to widen the separation. Not for production use.
