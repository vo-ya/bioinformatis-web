Educational simulator. Generates 100 proteins × 20 samples (10 case + 10 control) with 30% true-DE; missing values mix MNAR (low-abundance) and MAR (random). Imputation algorithms approximate published behavior; statistical methods are simplified versions of t-test, limma (empirical-Bayes shrinkage of variance), and DEqMS (PSM-count-weighted). Real workflows require careful preprocessing, normalisation, and biological interpretation. Not for clinical use.
