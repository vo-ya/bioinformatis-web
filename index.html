Course

Bioinformatics for Electrical Engineers

Spring 2026 · Vojislav Varjačić

A one-semester introduction to bioinformatics for students with an engineering background. The course treats genomics as an instrumentation, signal-processing, and machine-learning problem — which, at its core, is exactly what it is.

Each lecture is a single scrollable page with inline interactive artifacts. Artifacts also run standalone for live demos and self-study.
